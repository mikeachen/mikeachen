About Me

Since October 2015, I've been taking time off to build a life as an independent adult. So far, this has included learning to cook, getting fit, making friends outside of my professional network, exploring San Francisco, furnishing my apartment, learning about modern art, and more!

I've also been running Game of the Fortnight, a video game discussion group among the indie game developers at Gamenest. We play the same game for a fortnight (duh), then gather and discuss it! It's like a book club, but we play games.

Previously, I was a JavaScript Engineer building a web-based rich text editing environment at Inkling. Does contenteditable mean anything to you?

Find me on Twitter or shoot me an email.
