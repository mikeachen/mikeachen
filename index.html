Colleen Green: I Want to Grow Up

Colleen Green's 2015 LP helped me realize that growing up isn't about changing the way you act. It's a really important record to me, so I finally sat down and wrote a radio essay about it. Check it out if you've ever wondered when life is gonna feel the way it's supposed to!
